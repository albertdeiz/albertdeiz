# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 4, 2026*

## Overview

remeetner is a macOS application that reads your Google Calendar to detect upcoming Google Meet meetings and displays a fullscreen overlay when a meeting starts.

## Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. remeetner does not have a backend.

The app requests access to your Google Calendar solely to read event data. This data is:

- Processed locally on your device
- Never stored beyond the current session
- Never shared with any third party

OAuth tokens issued by Google are stored securely in your device's Keychain and are used only to authenticate requests to the Google Calendar API.

## Third-Party Services

remeetner uses the **Google Calendar API** to fetch your events. Your use of Google services is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

We do not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services.

## Data Retention

No data is retained by remeetner. Revoking access from your [Google Account permissions page](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions) removes all authorization immediately.
